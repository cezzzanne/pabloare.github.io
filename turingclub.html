Machine Learning / AI

At the Turing Club we believe in democratizing AI/ML knowledge on campus by creating a place for people to collaborate and learn. I am currently president of the club for second year in a row and guide +95% of the workshops.

We hosts workshops for students from all types of majors at UBC, where we teach them how to implement and understand ML algorithms. Thanks to the team at the Turing Club we have been able to hosts several workshops and events. Here are a couple of them:

Financial Analysis Workshop

Built an LSTM algorithm that predicted stock prices using Keras and Scikit-Learn. We used the amazing Google Collab as the environment

Image Recognition Workshop

We've hosted several Image Recognition workshops where we have trained Convolutional Neural Nets (CNN's) to predict faces and digits (MNIST). Used TensorFlow and Scikit-Learn, using a virtualenv and jupyter notebooks.

NLP Workshop

We used Keras and Google Collab to build an LSTM that generated Kanye West-like lyrics. We used as data all Kanye lyrics from ~2006 onwards (Data taken from Kaggle)
